Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Easy Ironing is a Telegram bot that analyzes clothing care labels and fabric composition to provide practical ironing instructions.

1. What Data We Receive

When you use the bot, we may receive:

  • Photos you send (care label images)
  • Text messages (fabric composition or commands)
  • Your Telegram user ID (provided by Telegram)

2. How Data Is Used

Your messages and images are processed to generate ironing instructions. Processing may involve third-party AI services.

3. Third-Party Services

We use external service providers, including:

  • Telegram (for messaging infrastructure)
  • Cloud hosting providers (for backend processing)
  • AI processing services (to analyze label information)

These providers may temporarily process the data necessary to deliver the service.

4. Data Storage

By default, we do not permanently store your images or personal messages. Temporary logs may exist for operational, debugging, or security purposes.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect the service and restrict unauthorized access.

6. Your Responsibility

Please do not submit sensitive personal information. The bot is designed only for garment care guidance.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact: contact@easyironing.chat
